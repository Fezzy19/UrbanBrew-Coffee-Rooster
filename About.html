Our Story

How It All Began

UrbanBrew Coffee Roasters was founded in 2023 by two coffee enthusiasts who wanted to bring specialty coffee to Johannesburg. After years of traveling through coffee-growing regions in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Brazil, they returned with a mission to create exceptional coffee experiences for their community.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, sustainably sourced coffee while educating our community about brewing techniques and coffee culture. We believe that great coffee should be accessible to everyone, and that each cup tells a story of the people and places that made it possible.

Ethical Sourcing

We work directly with farmers who practice sustainable agriculture and ensure fair wages for workers. Our commitment to ethical sourcing means that every bag of UrbanBrew coffee supports the communities that grow our beans, creating a positive impact from farm to cup.
